What We Do & Why We Do It

The UK faces an unprecedented environmental challenge in achieving its net-zero infrastructure targets. While renewables like wind and solar play a vital role, a modernized, zero-carbon network cannot safely operate on intermittency alone.

CNEI was founded to provide transparent, highly accessible resources that clarify the role of civil nuclear energy. We analyze safety metrics, lifecycle emissions, and grid mechanics to foster balanced, critical discussion across campus environments.

Our mission is to replace energy anxiety with objective scientific consensus. We champion data integrity, open debate, and technical literacy to prepare the next generation for the operational realities of our future grid.
