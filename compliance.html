Legal Disclosures & Privacy Architecture

1. Legal Entity Status

The Coalition of Nuclear Energy Initiatives (CNEI) is an independent, non-commercial unincorporated association and student-led educational campaign.

2. Privacy Notice & Data Protection

Under the UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018, this platform is engineered under a strict architectural directive of total Data Minimization:

  • Web Browsing: We deploy zero tracking cookies, execute no marketing analytics scripts, and host no user accounts. Your visit to this website is anonymous. Standard infrastructure security server logs (including visitor IP addresses) are handled directly by our hosting infrastructure provider, GitHub, to maintain network perimeter defenses.
  • Communications: If you choose to contact us via email or WhatsApp, your transmission information is strictly utilized to process your ongoing programmatic inquiry. We do not sell, share, or leverage personal data for marketing purposes.

3. Terms of Use & Informational Disclaimer

All content, data analysis, and briefings provided on this website are for general educational and informational purposes only. While CNEI strives for absolute scientific accuracy based on peer-reviewed sources, the information is provided "as is" without warranties of any kind. CNEI accepts no civil liability for operational decisions or structural policy interpretations derived from resources housed on this platform.

5. Accessibility Statement

CNEI is dedicated to inclusive digital access under the Equality Act 2010. The site features semantic HTML5 layouts, robust structural heading hierarchies, full keyboard navigation accessibility options, and validated high-contrast structural text properties across all presentation assets.
