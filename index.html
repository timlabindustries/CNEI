NUCLEAR SCIENCE FOR A STABLE FUTURE

The Coalition of Nuclear Energy Initiatives is an independent, student-led campaign analyzing the practical engineering realities of the UK's zero-carbon transition.

Scientific Literacy

We believe public policy should be driven by rigorous, peer-reviewed data and engineering realities, moving past sensationalized historical myths.

Grid Reliability

We believe a truly green UK power grid requires a stable, dependable baseline layer of nuclear power to support intermittent renewable infrastructure.

Environmental Stewardship

We believe in achieving deep, long-term carbon reduction without sacrificing economic stability or energy security.
